VINEET RADHAKRISHNAN



   An enthusiastic, practical and result driven professional holding a Masters degree in Business Administration, with 17 years of extensive customer centric experience that includes Product, Process and People Management. The key strength being aggressive, straight forward and a simplistic approach to all opportunities faced with immense perseverance.

   In 1928, he entered the University College Colombo and graduated with a first class honours degree from the University of London, gaining first place in the faculty of Science and winning the engineering scholarship. The scholarship enabled him to enter Downing College, Cambridge

   Specialties: Project Management, Product Management, Process Mining, Data Analytics ,Transition, Transformation, Migrations, Operations Management, Strategic Management, People Management, Stakeholder/Customer Management, Transition Experience, Networking & Relationship Building

   He received his education at the Royal College, Colombo, where he excelled in both academics and sports. He won the De Soysa Science Prize, the Mohamed Ali Arithmetic Prize, Evams Prize for Mathematics (shared with Danton G. Obyesekere), and he won house colours for cricket. J.R. Jayawardene, the future President of Sri Lanka, and Ceylon cricketer N.S. Joseph were his classmates at Royal College.
